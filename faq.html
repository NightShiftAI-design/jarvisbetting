FAQ

Planning clarity for a smoother inquiry.

These answers cover the most common questions clients ask before scheduling a tour or requesting a quote.

What is the guest capacity?

Celebration Event & Venue can accommodate up to 250 guests.

Is the venue indoor?

Yes. Celebration Event & Venue is an indoor event venue at 5335 Central Avenue Pike, Knoxville, TN 37912.

Are tables and chairs included?

Yes. Tables and chairs are included, helping simplify planning and reduce the number of rentals needed.

Is parking available?

Yes. Parking is available for guests.

Can I bring my own caterer?

Yes. Outside catering is allowed, and the venue does not require in-house-only catering.

Are food trucks allowed?

Yes. Food trucks can be used as part of your event plan.

Is alcohol allowed?

Yes. Alcohol is allowed with the appropriate permit.

Is setup and cleanup included?

Setup and cleanup support is available for an additional fee. Ask about this when requesting your quote.

Is a deposit required?

Yes. A deposit is required to reserve a date.

What is the minimum booking time?

The minimum booking time is 2 hours.

Can the venue host different styles of events?

Yes. The venue is suited for weddings, receptions, birthdays, corporate events, cultural events, and flexible custom gatherings.

How do I request a tour or quote?

Call us at 865.900.8800 to begin — share your preferred date, event type, and estimated guest count and we'll walk you through the next steps. You can also visit our contact page for additional details.

Have a date in mind?

Send your details and let the venue team confirm fit, availability, and next steps.
