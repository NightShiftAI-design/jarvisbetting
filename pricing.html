What shapes your quote

Every proposal is built around the event you are actually hosting.

01

Guest count

Expected attendance helps determine layout, flow, table needs, and overall room planning.

02

Event type

Weddings, receptions, birthdays, cultural events, and corporate gatherings have different timelines and room needs.

03

Setup needs

Decor, vendor access, ceremony transitions, entertainment, and custom layouts influence planning scope.

04

Timing

Minimum booking is 2 hours. Additional timing needs can be discussed during the proposal process.

05

Add-ons

Setup and cleanup support are available for an additional fee and can be included in your proposal.

06

Vendor plans

Outside catering, food trucks, alcohol permits, and vendor coordination may affect the event plan.
